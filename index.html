Marrying too young

Child marriage and its affects on girls' education

Child marriage is any formal or informal union or marriage between two people where one or both of the spouses are under the age of 18. Child marriage is a violation of human rights which affects primarily women and girls, although boys can also be affected.

For the period 2000-2011, just over one third (34%) of women aged 20 to 24 years in developing regions were married or in union before their eighteenth birthday. In 2010 this was equivalent to almost 67 million women. About 12 per cent of them were married or in union before age 15.
